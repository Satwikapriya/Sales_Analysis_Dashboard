# Sales_Analysis_Dashboard



 **Project Description**
This project analyzes sales data to identify key trends, top-performing products, and high-revenue regions.  
An interactive Power BI dashboard was created to help businesses make data-driven sales decisions.

---

**Dataset Information**
- Dataset: Superstore Sales Dataset  
- Records: ~9,800 rows  
- Data includes order details, customer segments, product categories, regions, and sales values.

---

 **Tools Used**
- Microsoft Excel (Data Cleaning)
- Power BI (Data Visualization & Dashboard)

---

 **Key Analysis Performed**
- Sales trend analysis over time
- Category-wise and product-wise sales analysis
- Region-wise sales performance
- KPI calculation (Total Sales, Total Orders, Average Sales)

---

**Business Insights & Recommendations**
- Sales peak during year-end months, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- The Consumer segment contributes the highest sales volume.
- Technology products generate the highest revenue.
- A small number of products drive the majority of total sales.

**Recommendations:**
- Increase inventory and marketing efforts during Q4.
- Focus promotions on top-performing products.
- Replicate successful strategies from high-performing regions in other regions.

---

 **Dashboard Preview**
The dashboard includes:
- KPI cards for quick performance summary
- Line chart for monthly sales trends
- Category and regional sales visualizations
- Interactive filters for better analysis
